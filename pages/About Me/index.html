Xudong Pan @ Fudan CS

Bio.

Welcome! My name is Xudong Pan (潘旭东). I am a 3rd-year PhD student in School of Computer Science, Fudan University.

I work at the interdisciplinary research area between AI and Security. In the past five years, I have published 10+ papers at top-tier conference/journals including TPAMI, TKDE, ICML, Security & Privacy (Oakland) and USENIX Security as the primary researcher.

Contact: xdpan18/a/t/fudan.edu.cn

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning Theory
  • Distributed Learning System
